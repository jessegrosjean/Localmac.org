About

I (Jesse Grosjean) run Hog Bay Software, a Mac OS X software company. For years I’ve felt guilty because I don’t release localized versions of my software, and I have even turned down free offers to localize my software.

I’m not alone. Most software isn’t localized, and no software is localized into all languages. Each new localization adds a new step the the software release process, and in most cases it’s just not worth the developers time to manage it.

I hope this site can provide a solution. My goal is get lazy developers (like myself) out of the way, and instead provide a site that allows users to create and share their own localizations anytime they are needed.

The following companies encourage you to create and post localizations of their software to this site. They will provide translators with a free license key, and will host the localized downloads if you need server space.

  • Hog Bay Software
  • Literature and Latte
